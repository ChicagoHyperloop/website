Sponsors


We are always willing to accept help with anything from pod components, design help, or even monetary assistance. Anything that can get us and a pod to the canadian hyperloop conference, spread the awareness of hyperloop technology or grow our organization.

For further sponsorship information, please reach out to us at chicagohyperloop@gmail.com.

As a sponsor, we will be sure to promote your company and brand through our social media and website, and we will also be sure to discuss you at our recruitment events. Furthermore, your contribution will be reflected in our pod design in the form of stickers on our carbon fiber shell for all to see in the Canadian Hyperloop Conference.

As visible from our pictures, we have plenty of space on the pod's carbon-fiber shell. We hope you fill that space!

*All benefits will last at least 3 years.
